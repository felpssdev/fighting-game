How to Play

As Player 1:

  • W - Jump
  • A - Move left
  • D - Move right
  • Space - Attack!

    • Samurai Mack is a little bit slower, but his attacks deal extra damage!


    Sir Arthur has good attack speed and damage, but his attack range is low!

    As Player 2:

  • ArrowUp - Jump
  • ArrowLeft - Move left
  • ArrowRight - Move right
  • ArrowDown - Attack!

    • Kenji is quicker and attacks faster, but his attacks deal less damage!
